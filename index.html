🎯 Practice Exercises

🤔 You have the network 192.168.0.0/24 and need 4 equal subnets. What subnet mask should you use?
255.255.255.0 (/24) - Keep it the same
255.255.255.128 (/25) - Split into 2 subnets
255.255.255.192 (/26) - Split into 4 subnets ✅
255.255.255.224 (/27) - Split into 8 subnets

🎓 Advanced Scenario

Real-World Challenge

You're the network admin for a company with 3 departments:

  • Sales: 50 employees (need 50 IP addresses)
  • Engineering: 25 employees (need 25 IP addresses)
  • Management: 10 employees (need 10 IP addresses)

Your ISP gave you: 192.168.100.0/24

Question: How would you subnet this efficiently?

💡 Solution Approach:

Sales Subnet
192.168.100.0/26
62 usable IPs
Engineering
192.168.100.64/27
30 usable IPs
Management
192.168.100.96/28
14 usable IPs
